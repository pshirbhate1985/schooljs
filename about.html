All about our school !!


My school is one of the best and most beautiful schools in the city. It has a large and modern building with many bright classrooms. The school campus has beautiful green gardens with colorful flowers . Our teachers are very kind, loving, and helpful to all students . They teach us how to read, write, draw, and solve math problems.My school has a very big playground where we play during recess.I love to play football, badminton, and hide-and-seek with my friends.There is a quiet library filled with many interesting storybooks.We also have a computer lab where we learn to use computers. We also have a science lab to see interesting experiments.I have a lot of wonderful classmates, and we always help each other.We sit together during lunch break and happily share our food boxes. We celebrate grand festivals, Independence Day, and Republic Day at school.The school hosts an annual sports meet where students win shiny medals.My school makes learning feel like a wonderful and joyful adventure.It is like a second home where I feel safe, happy, and cared for.I love my school very much and feel truly proud to study here.
